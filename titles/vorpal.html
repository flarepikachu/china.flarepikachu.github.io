Vorpal

Laced with the power of darkness and destruction, the Vorpal Sword pierces through enemies with ease.

Vorpal is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 180-200

Crit Chance: 35%

Crit Multiplier: 200%

Health Bonus: 300

Speed Bonus: 14%

EPS Bonus: -3/s

EPH Bonus: +7/hit

Passive Ability

Every 5th melee hit deals 7x damage, bypassing damage reduction.

Trivia
  • Title name and ability concept suggested by Flare.
