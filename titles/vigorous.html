Vigorous

Blessings and wine are the two ways to bestow vigor to the people.

Vigorous is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 140-170

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 180%

Health Bonus: 600

Speed Bonus: 10%

EPS Bonus: +4/s

Passive Ability

+10 energy per second for 10 seconds after using 400 energy. Can be triggered every 30 seconds.

Trivia
  • Title name and ability concept suggested by Flare.
