Divine

The kingdom wishfully waited for the divine intervention that failed to arrive.

Divine is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 100-120

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 175%

Health Bonus: 600

Speed Bonus: 5%

EPS Bonus: +5/s

EPH Bonus: -13/hit

Skill 暴击率: 5%

Passive Ability

Gain a 15% damage boost for 10 seconds after hitting 40 targets. Can be triggered every 30 seconds.

Trivia
  • Title name and ability concept suggested by Flare.
