Enhanced

The last person that heckled an Enhancer got thwacked with years of crippling pain and silence.

Enhanced is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 155-180

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 180%

Health Bonus: 400

Speed Bonus: 8%

EPS Bonus: +3/s

Passive Ability

Increase the duration of effects caused by primary (right-click) abilities by 2s.

Effects that are affected by this title include:

  • Burn
  • Slowness
  • Wounding
  • Bleeding
  • Cripple
  • Silence
  • Leech

Trivia
