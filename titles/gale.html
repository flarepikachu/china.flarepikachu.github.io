Gale

Run with the wind, Bullseye!

Gale is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 150-170

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 185%

Health Bonus: 500

Speed Bonus: 20%

Passive Ability

Increase movement speed by 40% and decrease energy consumption of all abilities by 10 for 10 seconds. Can be triggered every 30 seconds.

Trivia
  • Title name and ability concept suggested by Soupy.
