Gradient

Taste the rainbow! - Skittles

Gradient is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 140-170

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 160%

Health Bonus: 700

Speed Bonus: 10%

EPS Bonus: +3/s

EPH Bonus: +3/hit

Skill Crit Multiplier: 20%

Passive Ability

Perpetually regenerate 5% of your health every 5 seconds.

Trivia
  • Title name and ability concept suggested by Plikie.
