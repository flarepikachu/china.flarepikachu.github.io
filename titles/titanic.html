Titanic

With great size comes great power and responsiblity.

Titanic is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 130-150

暴击率: 15%

暴击加成: 160%

Health Bonus: 1500

Speed Bonus: 5%

Passive Ability

Increase maximum health by 1% per upgrade purchased.

Trivia
  • Title name and ability concept suggested by Flare.
