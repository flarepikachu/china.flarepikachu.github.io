Rarity & Titles

There are currently four rarities for weapons, being Common, Rare, Epic and Legendary.

Common, Rare, and Epic weapons have a range of stat distributions. The stats for Common, Rare and Epic weapons are generated upon being dropped from mobs. Once weapon stats are generated, they cannot be changed. Warlords 2 PvE does not feature a weapon stat reroll system. If you’re looking for a higher weapon score, you will need to grind for new weapons for a chance at a higher weapon score.

On the other hand, Legendary weapons feature a fixed stat distribution based on the weapon’s title, with no RNG involved. For example, the weapon Vorpal Cornelius of the Berserker features stat distributions based on the weapon title ‘Vorpal’.
