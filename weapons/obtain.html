Obtaining Weapons

As of Warlords 2 PvE release, the only way to get Common, Rare and Epic weapons is to play games of Warlords 2 PvE. Weapons are dropped at a 1% rate of 90%/9%/1% from basic mobs and at a 5% rate of 75%/20%/5% from elite/boss mobs.

Legendary weapons do not currently drop from mobs. Method of obtaining unknown? =)
