Synthetic Shards

Synthetic Shards can be obtained by salvaging Common, Rare, and Epic weapons. Synthetic Shards can also be obtained through Level Rewards and Susan.

Synthetic Shards are part of the cost to purchase and upgrade weapons. Synthetic Shards are currently involved in the following transactions:

  • Purchasing a Legendary Weapon
  • Upgrading Epic and Legendary Weapons
  • Applying Titles to Legendary Weapons
  • Applying Star Pieces to Legendary Weapons

Synthetic Shards may be part of other (non-weapon) costs in the future.
