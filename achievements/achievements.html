Achievements

Achievements have been added to Warlords 2 to commemorate your greatest accomplishments. You can access the achievement menu with the command /achievements.

There are two types of achievements, being public and hidden achievements.

Public achievements have their descriptions available to read in the achievement menu before unlocking the achievement.

Hidden achievements only have their achievement descriptions available after you have obtained that achievement.

Achievements can have varying levels of difficulty. This will soon be indicated on the achievement description.
