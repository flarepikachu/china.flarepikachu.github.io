Illusion Vanguard Update - v1.1
Released November 11th 2022
Wave Defense
Easy Mode NEW!

  • -25% Mob Health
  • -25% Mob Damage
  • -25% Mob Spawns

  • Insignia gain has been increased by 50%
  • Slow Scaling
    • Exiled and Void enemies do not appear.
    • Most elite enemies appear at a lower rate.
Hard Mode NEW!

  • +50% Mob Health
  • +50% Mob Damage

  • No Respawns
    • Only way to respawn on death is by clearing the wave.
  • Quick Scaling
    • Several basic mobs have been replaced by their elite upgrade.
    • High chance for Exiled and Void enemies.
    • Bosses have additional or augmented abilities.
General

  • You now get an additional master upgrade at wave 50 and 100 respectively in Endless.
  • Raised barrier walls on Illusion Crossfire.
  • Monsters left counter on scoreboard will now display red while it's still spawning mobs.
  • Monster count per wave is now the same for 3 and 4 player games.
  • Add minimum requirement of 10 games before showing up on weekly leaderboards.
  • Add minimum requirement of 50 games before showing up on Lifetime leaderboards.
  • Added Star Piece Synthesizer, allowing Star Pieces to be combined into higher rarities.
  • All negative weapon attributes will no longer be upgraded.
  • Condensed Masterworks Fair /myposition.
  • Added PvE difficulty to leaderboards.
  • Disabled inviting players to guild if guild is full.
  • Added guild MOTD.
  • Fixed guild invites not expiring (for reinvites)
  • Added guild leaderboard values next to guild.
  • Fixed guild leaderboards not refreshing after game.
  • Cleaned up guild leaderboard message.

Class Balance

法师
  • 时空断裂's healing upgrade branch has been changed from 3/6/9/12% to 5/10/15/20%.

火系法师
  • Fiery Fusilade's (火球术 Master) max health damage now deals a minimum of 100 damage and max 800 damage. (can be increased by damage modifiers.)
  • 火球术's energy upgrade branch has been changed from 2.5/5/7.5/10 to 5/10/15/20.

冰系法师
  • 寒冰屏障's duration upgrade branch has been changed from 2/4/6/8s to 1/2/3/4s.

灵魂近卫
  • 陨落之魂' damage upgrade branch has been changed from 2.5/5/7.5/10% to 7.5/15/22.5/30%.
  • 陨落之魂' energy upgrade branch has been changed from 2/3/4/5 to 2.5/5/7.5/10.
  • 锁魂之械's bind duration upgrade branch has been changed from 0.5/1/1.5/2s to 1/2/3/4s.
  • 死亡债务 will now aggro mobs within its radius.
  • Ghoul's Gamble (死亡债务 Master) will now reduce damage taken by 50% instead of 25%.

大地守护神
  • Reduced Earthen Rupture's (大地刺枪 Master) damage by 50% and it can no longer hit critical hits.
  • Changed Terrestrial Meteor (巨石投掷 Master)'s cooldown from being 3x to 2x the original.
  • Changed Terrestrial Meteor (巨石投掷 Master)'s energy cost from being 2x to 1.5x the original.
  • Loamliving Weapon (大地之械 Master) now heals for 10% missing health instead of 5% and energy scaling based on missing health has been doubled.
  • 治愈图腾's range upgrade branch has been changed from 1/2/3/4 to 2/4/6/8 blocks.

战士
  • Added 7.5/15/22.5/30% damage upgrade to 大地锤击's upgrade branches.
  • Earthen Tremor (大地锤击 Master) now deals 150% of the original damage on landing instead of 200%.

狂战士
  • Lacerating Strike (创伤之斩 Master) now deals 80% more damage against bleeding targets instead of 50%, max health damage increased from 0.25% to 0.5%. (min 100, max 800 damage.)
  • 创伤之斩's damage upgrade branch has been changed from 3.75/7.5/11.25/15% to 7.5/15/22.5/30%.
  • 地震波's damage upgrade branch has been changed from 3.75/7.5/11.25/15% to 7.5/15/22.5/30%.
  • Sanguineous (嗜血 Master) now increases damage dealt against wounded/bleeding targets by 40% instead of 20%, duration increase changed from 3s to 5s.
  • Maniacal Rage (狂暴 Master) now no longer increases damage taken and increased crit chance/crit multiplier cap from 30% to 50%.

防御者
  • 创伤之斩's damage upgrade branch has been changed from 3.75/7.5/11.25/15% to 7.5/15/22.5/30%.
  • 地震波's damage upgrade branch has been changed from 3.75/7.5/11.25/15% to 7.5/15/22.5/30%.
  • Intersection (护卫 Master) now removes the damage cap of 护卫 and reduces damage dealt to the 防御者 from 50% to 20%.
  • Final Stand (背水一战 Master) will now draw aggro from mobs when cast and will knock them back continuously instead of just once.

亡魂领主
  • 生命之球 will now target least healthy allies first.
  • Relentless Army (不死军团 Master) now deals 458 - 612 (+2% enemy max health) damage instead of 68-102 (+0.5% enemy max health).

刺客
  • 裁决之斩's healing on kill upgrade has been changed from 75/150/225/300 to 100/200/300/400.
  • 燃烧诅咒 will now blind mobs for double the duration, making them take longer to aggro onto a new player.
  • Shadow Stagger (暗影步伐 Master) will now increase speed and knockback resistance by 80% for 5 seconds instead of 20% for 3 seconds. (Description was incorrect in general.)

维护者
  • 正义之斩's base damage has been increased by 30%.
  • Righteous Slash (正义之斩 Master) now hits 4 targets instead of 2 and silences all targets hit for 4 seconds.
  • Conscience Crush (灵魂枷锁 Master) now hits up to 8 targets instead of 5 and increases silence duration by 3s as well.
  • Deflective Tesseract (棱镜护盾 Master) now increases passive damage reduction by 25% instead of increasing max health by 20%.
  • Reflective Barrier (博平反正 Master) no longer requires line of sight of enemies to reflect damage.

药剂师
  • Liquor of Life (活力金酒 Master) now doubles the duration of the bonus energy per second and slows all enemies hit by 30% for 3 seconds.

Mob Balance

General
  • Blazes have been removed (for now).
  • Wolves have been removed (for now).
  • Magma cubes will no longer split in baby magma cubes and the damage reduction no longer stacks with multiple magma cubes.

Narmer
  • Everytime you kill an Acolyte it will now tell you what the Death Wish kill window is.
  • Narmer's Acolytes will now switch player aggro if possible every 10 seconds.

Mithra
  • Mithra will no longer periodically spawn Lunar Venaris (Spiders).
  • 灵魂交换 ability has been removed.
  • Mithra will now use 火焰喷发 barrage every 10 seconds, with a 2 second delay and warning sound.

Zenith
  • Base health reduced by 6%~. (On a 4 player scale this will make the difference between 130k and 110k HP total.)
  • Cleanse cooldown increased from 2s to 4s.
  • Armageddon will now play a sound cue before activating, along with a 2 second delay.
  • Armageddon's delay between shockwaves has been increased from 0.25s to 0.6s.
    • Hit radius has been reduced from 8/15/24 to 10/15/20.
    • Damage has been increased by 50%.
  • Zenith will now stop moving when casting Armageddon.
  • Zenith's attacks will now deal less knockback on hit.
  • Zenith's Envoy Legionaires will now heal Zenith for 500 health every 5 seconds while alive.
    • Envoy Legionaires' base health has been reduced by 6%~ but base damage increased by 200%.
  • Zenith will no longer periodically spawn Illusion Illuminations (Magma Cubes).

  • Fixed reducing damage reduction on some mobs to cause them to take too much damage.
  • Fixed quests not showing after reset.
  • Fixed weekly quests not resetting after reset.
  • Fixed 浪漫链接 sometimes setting ally health to 0 permanently when breaking the chain.
  • Fixed Capactior Totem master not increasing range as indicated by description.
  • Fixed 超导图腾 master incorrectly increasing damage taken by enemies per proc.
  • Fixed unlocked weapon skins resetting after swapping titles.
  • Fixed Masterworks Fair not resetting.
  • Fixed strike Execute not granting strike healing.
  • Fixed Holy Imbusion (守护者's 圣光倾注 master) to not properly reset cooldown on cast.
  • Fixed teammate achievement unlock hover messages being white on new line.
