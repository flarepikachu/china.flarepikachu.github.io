Illusion Vanguard Hotfix - v1.0.4
Wave Defense
General

  • Added an enchantment effect on classes with unclaimed rewards.

Weapons

  • Assists can now drop weapons at half the chance of kills.
  • Added basic sounds to all weapon interactions.
  • Your bound weapons in your weapon inventory are now enchanted.
  • Added more filters to weapons menu. You can now filter by selected spec and by bound weapons instead of having to scavenge for that one weapon!!
  • Added an option to salvage all weapons.
    • This feature is currently Patreon-only.
      • You can subscribe to our Patreon here.
      • All donations will be used to pay for costs associated with running the server itself.
    • You can use filters to select which weapons you want to salvage.
    • This feature supports an option to ignores all weapons with a weaponscore that is 70 or higher.
      • The weaponscore parameter for salvage all cannot be modified yet.
    • Killed Chessking

Guilds

  • Increased Base Guild Coin Conversion to 5%.
  • Increased Guild Coin Conversion Upgrade from 5% to 10%.
  • Decreased the costs of guild upgrades over the board.
  • Fixed Guild Leaderboard starting at 0.
  • Fixed Guild Upgrade lore.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed 法师's Arcane Aegis (奥术护盾 Master) not activating on shield break.
  • Fixed 刺客's Killing Order (裁决之斩 Master) leaving mobs on 0 HP.
  • Added 's' to Legend Fragments in sidebar
