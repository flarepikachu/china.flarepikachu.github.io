Illusion Vanguard Hotfix - v1.0.2
Wave Defense
General

  • Tripled the experience gain of classes from PvE.
  • Weapon shard amount on salvage has been tripled. The new values are as follows:
    • Common: 1-2 > 4-6
    • Rare: 6-9 > 10-12
    • Epic: 12-18 > 42-48
  • Fixed tiered achievements not being awarded to players.
  • Added hoverable upgrade descriptions in the chat. (Useful to see what your teammates are getting).
  • Changed the color of list of players in game on spectator menu.
  • Fixed bot wave message in the server status PvE game description.
  • Killed Chessking

Class Balance

刺客
  • 刺客's 绞杀密令 now properly reduces the cooldown of Blinding Assault.
  • 刺客's Death Strike (裁决之斩 Master) now awards the player with kill credit instead of an assist. This allows the class to properly synergize with it's ultimate ability.
  • 刺客's Killing Order (绞杀密令 Master) now reduces active cooldowns by 3s instead of 1s.
守护者
  • 守护者's 神圣光辉 Tier IV Alleviate now has -30 Energy Cost in addition to 40% Healing Increase.
  • 守护者's 神圣光辉 Tier IV Zeal now has 20% 冷却 Reduction instead of 10% 冷却 Reduction.
药剂师
  • FINALLY fixed 药剂师's right click master to deal double damage instead of triple.
  • 药剂师's Crystalizing Chains (浪漫链接 Master) health and damage bonus changed from 30% to 25%.
大地守护神
  • Earthen Rupture (大地刺枪 Master) now only hits and slows one enemy (instead of AOE) and the damage has been decreased slightly.
  • 大地守护神's Terrestrial Meteor (巨石投掷 Master) damage reduced from 5x to 4x.

Mob Balance

  • There is now a hint message on how to beat Narmer.
  • Acolyte's Revenge range reduced from 20 to 15 blocks.
  • Fixed Zenith's Armageddon hitting 刺客 while invisible.
