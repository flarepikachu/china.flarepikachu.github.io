Supply Drop Susan

This cute little bunny will give you great rewards if you feed her Supply Drop tokens! Supply Drop tokens can be obtained primarily through the Masterworks Fair. Supply Drop tokens are also available from Level Rewards and can be purchased for 10,000 Coins.

The prized rewards from the Supply Drop are the Star Pieces and the Skill Boost Modifier. The drop rates as of release are as follows:
