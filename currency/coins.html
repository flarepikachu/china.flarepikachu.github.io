Coins

Coins are the generic, base currency in Warlords 2 PvE. Most transactions in PvE will involve coins as the primary or secondary cost to unlocking or upgrading.

Coins are primarily gained by playing the game. A fully cleared game of Wave Defense at Normal difficulty awards 6,900 coins before multipliers.

Coins can also be obtained through level rewards. More information about level rewards can be found here.

As mentioned in the patch notes, notable costs include:

  • $1,000,000 as part of the legendary weapon unlock cost,
  • $500,000 as the guild creation cost. (You also need 20 PvE clears to create a guild).
