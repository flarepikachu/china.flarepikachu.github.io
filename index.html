Warlords 2 PvE

Welcome to the documentation page for Warlords 2 PvE! This is work in progress and there are no promises that this will get updated regularly. I hope this documentation gives you better insight towards the development of Warlords 2 PvE and the new features added.

Development for Warlords 2 PvE started around May 2022. The initial idea was to opt for wave defense, where players would be working together to defend a central target from enemy attacks. Instead, we have opted for a simpler wave survival gamemode akin to Hypixel Zombies, albeit with both in-game and out-of-game upgrades and many more features to hopefully keep the game interesting.
