Levels & Rewards

The PvE Update comes with the implementation of gaining spec experience through PvE games and the introduction of Level Rewards.

For Normal Mode, players will gain 20 EXP per wave cleared, with a 400 EXP bonus if wave 25 is cleared.

For Endless, players will gain 20 EXP per wave cleared.

Class and spec levels gained from our PvP gamemodes will carry over to PvE. For players who have played an extensive amount of competitive and/or public games on our server, this means that you likely have many level rewards for you to claim.

You receive rewards for each level up. You can access these rewards by pressing the player skull on your 8th slot.
