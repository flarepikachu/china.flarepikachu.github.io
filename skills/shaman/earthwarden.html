大地守护神

以治疗为导向的萨满专业，召唤地球的力量来粉碎敌人并帮助盟友。

大地刺枪

能量消耗: 100

暴击率: 15%

暴击加成: 175%

Send forth an underground earth spike that locks onto a targeted enemy player. When the spike reaches its target it emerges from the ground, dealing 404 - 562 damage to any nearby enemies and launches them up into the air.

Has an initial cast 范围 of 10 blocks.

巨石投掷

冷却: 7.1s

能量消耗: 80

暴击率: 15%

暴击加成: 175%

Launch a giant boulder that shatters and deals 451 - 673 damage to all enemies near the impact point and knocks them back slightly.

大地之械

冷却: 15.7s

能量消耗: 30

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 200%

Imbue your weapon with the power of the earth, causing each of your melee attacks to have a 40% chance to heal you and 2 nearby allies for 240% weapon damage. Lasts 8 seconds.

连锁治愈

冷却: 8s

能量消耗: 40

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 175%

Discharge a beam of energizing lightning that heals you and a targeted friendly player for 533 - 719 health and jumps to 1 additional target within 10 blocks.

Each ally healed reduces the cooldown of 巨石投掷 by 2.5 seconds.

Has an initial cast 范围 of 15 blocks.

治愈图腾

冷却: 62.6s

能量消耗: 40

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 175%

Place a totem on the ground that pulses constantly, 治愈 nearby allies in a 7 block radius for 191 - 224 health every second. The 治愈 will gradually提升 by 25% (up to 210%) every second. Lasts 6 seconds.

Pressing SHIFT or reactivating the ability will cause your totem to pulse with immense force, crippling all enemies for 6 seconds. Crippled enemies deal 25% less damage.
