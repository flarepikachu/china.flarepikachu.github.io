灵魂近卫

以防御为导向的萨满专业，使用灵魂的帮助来减免伤害和逃离死亡的厄运。

陨落之魂

能量消耗: 55

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 180%

Summon a wave of fallen souls, dealing 164 - 212 damage to all enemies they pass through. Each target hit reduces the cooldown of 灵魂链接 by 2 seconds.

Has a maximum 范围 of 35 blocks.

灵魂链接

冷却: 8.6s

能量消耗: 40

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 175%

Links your spirit with up to 3 enemy players, dealing 236.2 - 446.2 damage to the first target hit. Each additional hit deals 20% reduced damage. You gain 40% speed for 1.5 seconds, and take 15% reduced damage for 4.5 seconds.

Has an initial cast 范围 of 20 blocks.

锁魂之械

冷却: 21.9s

能量消耗: 30

Your melee attacks BIND enemies for 2 seconds. Against BOUND targets, your next 灵魂链接 will heal you for 400 health (half for 2 nearby allies). Your next 陨落之魂 will reduce the cooldown of all abilities by 1.5s. (0.5s for 2 nearby allies). Both buffs may be activated for each melee hit. Lasts 12 seconds.

Successful soulbind procs will grant you 25% 击退强度 resistance for 1.2 seconds. (Max 3.6 seconds).

Active instances are now capped at two.

反击意志

冷却: 31.32s

能量消耗: 20

Taking damage empowers your damaging abilities and melee hits, restoring health and energy based on 10 + 10% of the damage you've recently took. Lasts 12 seconds.

死亡债务

冷却: 70.5

能量消耗: 20

Spirits' Respite: Place down a totem that delays 100% of incoming damage towards yourself. Transforms into 死亡债务 after 4-6 seconds (提升s with higher health), or when you exit its 10 block radius.

死亡债务: Take 100% of the damage delayed by Spirit's Respite over 6 seconds. The totem will heal nearby allies for 15% of all damage that you take. If you survive, deal 15% of the damage delayed to nearby enemies. Take aggro of all mobs within the radius of 死亡债务.

Successful Soulbind procs on enemies add 0.5s to your totem duration. (Cap of 6s).

Changelog

Illusion Vanguard 1.1

  • 陨落之魂' damage upgrade branch has been changed from 2.5/5/7.5/10% to 7.5/15/22.5/30%.
  • 陨落之魂' energy upgrade branch has been changed from 2/3/4/5 to 2.5/5/7.5/10.
  • 锁魂之械's bind duration upgrade branch has been changed from 0.5/1/1.5/2s to 1/2/3/4s.
  • 死亡债务 will now aggro mobs within its radius.
  • Ghoul's Gamble (死亡债务 Master) will now reduce damage taken by 50% instead of 25%.

Illusion Vanguard 1.0

  • Added the 灵魂近卫 page to the documentation.
