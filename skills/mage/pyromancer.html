火系法师

以伤害为导向的法师专业，使用破坏性的火焰法术消灭敌人。

火球术

能量消耗: 70

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 175%

Shoot a fireball that will explode for 334.4 - 433.4 damage. A direct hit will cause the enemy to take an additional 15% extra damage. Has an optimal 范围 of 50 blocks.

火焰喷发

冷却: 9.4s

能量消耗: 60

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 185%

Launch a flame burst that will explode for 557 - 753 damage. The 暴击率提升s by 1% for each travelled block, with a 100% 暴击率 maximum.

时空断裂

冷却: 28.2s

能量消耗: 30

Activate to place a time rune on the ground. After 5 seconds, you will warp back to that location and restore 30% of your health.

奥术护盾

冷却: 31.3s

能量消耗: 40

Surround yourself with arcane energy, creating a shield that will absorb incoming damage for up to 50% of your maximum health. Lasts 6 seconds.

炼狱

冷却: 47s

Combust into a molten inferno, increasing your 暴击率 and 暴击加成 by 30%. Lasts 18 seconds.
