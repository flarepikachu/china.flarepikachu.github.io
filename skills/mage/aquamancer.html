水系法师

以治疗为导向的法师专业，使用水系法术治疗盟友并对敌人造成轻微伤害。这个专业可在友方满血时额外回复友方最多10%的生命值。

涌泉术

能量消耗: 80

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 175%

Shoot a bolt of water that will burst for 231 - 299 damage and restore 315 - 434 health to allies. A direct hit will heal for 15% extra damage or 治愈.

Has an optimal 范围 of 40 blocks.

涌泉术 can overheal allies for up to 10% of their max health as bonus health. The bonus health lasts for 15 seconds.

水之吐息

冷却: 6.3s

能量消耗: 60

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 175%

Breathe water in a cone in front of you, knocking back enemies, cleansing all debuffs, and restoring 528 - 723 health to yourself and all allies hit.

水之吐息 can overheal allies for up to 10% of their max health as bonus health. The bonus health lasts for 15 seconds.

时空断裂

冷却: 28.2s

能量消耗: 30

Activate to place a time rune on the ground. After 5 seconds, you will warp back to that location and restore 30% of your health.

奥术护盾

冷却: 31.3s

能量消耗: 40

Surround yourself with arcane energy, creating a shield that will absorb incoming damage for up to 50% of your maximum health. Lasts 6 seconds.

治愈之雨

冷却: 52.8s

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 200%

Conjure rain at targeted location that will restore 100-125 health every 0.5 seconds to allies. Lasts 12 seconds.

You may move 治愈之雨 to your location by pressing your shift key or activating your 治愈 Rain again.

治愈之雨 can overheal allies for up to 10% of their max health as bonus health. The bonus health lasts for 15 seconds.
