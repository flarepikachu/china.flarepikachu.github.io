药剂师

A 治愈-oriented spec that uses special brews and the chemical powers to weaken their foes and aid allies.

贯穿之刺

能量消耗: 90

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 175%

Impale an enemy, dealing 323 - 427 damage and afflict them with the LEECH effect for 5 seconds. Whenever an ally (excluding yourself) deals damage to a leeched enemy, they heal for 25% of the damage dealt. You heal for 15% of the damage you deal to a leeched enemy instead.

舒缓酏剂

冷却: 7s

能量消耗: 60

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 175%

Throw a short 范围 elixir bottle. The bottle will shatter upon impact, 治愈 nearby allies for 551 - 648 health and damage nearby enemies for 235 - 342 damage. The projectile will form a small puddle that heals allies for 158 - 204 health per second. Lasts 4 seconds.

活力金酒

冷却: 14s

能量消耗: 30

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 175%

Discharge a shockwave of special potions around you, 治愈 allies in the 范围 for 359-485 health.

Each enemy afflicted with your LEECH effect within the 范围 will cause the enemy to discharge an additional shockwave of vitality that heals 2 nearby allies for 268 - 324 health and提升s their energy regeneration by 15 for 3s.

浪漫链接

冷却: 16s

能量消耗: 50

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 200%

Bind yourself to 3 allies near you, increasing the damage they deal by 12% as long as the link is active. Lasts 8 seconds.

When the link expires, you and the allies are healed for 728 - 815 health. Breaking the link early will only heal allies for 12.5% of the original amount for each second they have been linked.

The link will break if you are 15 blocks apart.

致命毒雾

冷却: 50s

能量消耗: 40

Summon a toxin-filled cloud around you, poisoning all enemies inside the area. Poisoned enemies take 50 + 4% of their max health as damage per second, for 5 seconds. Enemies poisoned by your 致命毒雾 are slowed by 25% for 3 seconds on cast.

Each enemy hit will be afflicted with LEECH for 5 seconds.
