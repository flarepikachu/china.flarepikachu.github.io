维护者

以防御为导向的侠盗专业。善于禁止敌方的攻击并使用正义的力量来保护盟友。

正义之斩

能量消耗: 90

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 175%

Strike the targeted enemy for 535.6 - 679.9 damage. Each strike reduces the duration of your struck target's active ability timers by 0.5 seconds.

Additionally, if your struck target is silenced, reduce the cooldown of your 棱镜护盾 by 0.8 seconds and reduce their active ability timers by 0.8 seconds instead.

灵魂枷锁

冷却: 9s

能量消耗: 40

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 175%

Shackle up to 1 enemy and deal 446 - 589 damage. Shackled enemies are silenced for 2 - 3.5 seconds, making them unable to use their main attack for the duration. The silence duration提升s by 0.5 seconds for every 500 damage you took in the last 6 seconds. Gain a short burst of 40% movement speed for 1.5 seconds after shackling an enemy.

Has an optimal 范围 of 15 blocks.

Shackled enemies cannot melee for the duration of silence in PvE.

心心相印

冷却: 12s

能量消耗: 20

Throw a chain towards an ally in a 15 block radius, grappling yourself towards the targeted ally. You and the targeted ally gain VIND for 6 seconds, granting immunity to debuffs. You are healed for 600 health after reaching your ally.

心心相印's 范围 is greatly reduced when holding a flag.

棱镜护盾

冷却: 26s

能量消耗: 40

Create a bubble shield around you that lasts 6s. All projectiles that pass through the barrier have their damage reduced by 75%.

After 6s, the bubble will burst, 治愈 you and all allies for 200 + 1.5% missing health and grant 3% damage reduction per hit taken (max 30%) while 棱镜护盾 was active.

博平反正

冷却: 50s

能量消耗: 60

All allies within an 8 block radius gain the status VIND, which clears all de-buffs. In addition, the status VIND prevents allies from being affected by de-buffs and grants 50% 击退强度 resistance for 12s. You gain 30% damage reduction for 8s.
