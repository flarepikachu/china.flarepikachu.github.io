A damage-oriented 侠盗 specialization that is a master of stealth, rapidly taking out any enemies who cross them.

能量消耗: 70

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 185%

Strike the targeted enemy, dealing 326 - 441 damage. Every fourth attack is a guaranteed critical strike. Critical strikes temporarily提升 your movement speed by 25% for 2 seconds.

冷却: 8s

能量消耗: 60

暴击率: 25%

暴击加成: 175%

Ignite the targeted area with a cross flame, dealing 408 - 552 damage. Enemies hit are blinded for 2 seconds.

暗影步伐

冷却: 12s

能量消耗: 20

暴击率: 15%

暴击加成: 175%

Leap forward, dealing 466 - 598 damage to all enemies close on cast or when landing on the ground. You take reduced fall damage while leaping.

暗影步伐 has reduced 范围 when holding a Flag.

灵魂交换

冷却: 30s

能量消耗: 40

Switch locations with an enemy, blinding them for 1.5 seconds. Has an optimal 范围 of 13 blocks. 灵魂交换 has low vertical 范围.

绞杀密令*

冷却: 50s

能量消耗: 60

Cloak yourself for 7 seconds, granting you movement speed and making you invisible to the enemy for the duration. However, taking up to 600 fall damage or any type of ability damage will end your invisibility.

All your attacks against an enemy will mark them vulnerable. Vulnerable enemies take 130% more damage. Additionally, enemies hit from behind take an additional 140% more damage.

Killing your mark will decrease the cooldown of your 暗影步伐 by 2s and Order of Eviscerate by 8 seconds.
