亡魂领主

A support-oriented 战士 specialization that can give allies a second chance of life.

致残打击

能量消耗: 100

暴击率: 15%

暴击加成: 200%

Strike the targeted enemy, dealing 362.25 - 498 damage and crippling them for 3 seconds. A crippled player deals 10% less damage for the duration of the effect. Adds 5% less damage dealt per additional strike (Max 20%).

无畏冲锋

冷却: 9.32s

能量消耗: 60

暴击率: 20%

暴击加成: 200%

Charge forward, dealing 457 - 601 damage to all enemies you pass through. Enemies hit are immobolized, preventing movement for 0.5 seconds.

大地锤击

冷却: 9.32s

能量消耗: 30

暴击率: 35%

暴击加成: 200%

Slam the ground, creating a shockwave around you that deals 326 - 441 damage and knocks enemies back slightly.

生命之球

冷却: 19.57s

能量消耗: 20

Spawn two orbs on initial cast. Striking and hitting enemies with abilities causes them to drop an orb of life that lasts 8 seconds, restoring 225 health to the ally that picks it up and nearby allies. Orbs that remain on the ground will提升 in 治愈 after 1.5s by 40% over 6.5s. 生命之球 lasts 14 seconds.

You may SNEAK to make the orbs levitate towards you or the nearest ally in a 20 block radius. 生命之球 targets least healthy allies first when using SNEAK.

不死军团

冷却: 62.64s

能量消耗: 60

You may chain up to 6 allies in a 15 block radius to heal them for 100 + 3.5% of their missing health every second. Lasts 10 seconds.

While the ability is active, chained allies that take fatal damage will be revived with 100% of their max health and energy. Revived allies rapidly take 10% of their max health as damage every second.
